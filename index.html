A Little About Myself

My name is Chris Jaeger, and I am a Visual Communication Design Motion Track at Stevenson University. I am a beginner at web design, but learning more everyday, and I am proficient with Illustrator and Photoshop. Drawing is my specialty and I try to incorporate my illustrations whenever I see fit. I'd like to one day work at a design firm and be able to use my skills and talents that I've learned over the years at Stevenson.
