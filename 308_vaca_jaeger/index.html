Thrill Rides of the Sea

A water park like no other. Test your capabilities against Poseidon, a God as powerful as a tsunami and unpredictable as a hurrican. Can you handle the Wrath of the Sea God's Trident? Will you be able out run a raging typhoon? Or keep you head dry in the deceiving Siren's River. All of this and more awaits you if you dare to dive in at Poseidon Water Park.
