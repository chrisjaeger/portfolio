OUR STORY

Go-Go's Retread Threads is an amazing mobile vintage and consignment store!!
A bus which holds fashion wonders, stylistic delights and sometimes candy. We have something for everyone, from a classic j crew blazer to a sweet little anthropologie frock to some really rockin' vintage boots! All at ridiculously great prices. We travel to markets, festivals, music venues, bars, colleges, and your street to sell our goods to people who want to buy fabulous affordable fashion.

Stacey

Stacey is the one and only owner of Go-Go's Threads. She travels the world finding the best vintage clothing at the best price, because Stacey believes that you don't have to spend a lot of money to be fashionable and express yourself. Besides making life-long friendships with her customers, Stacey enjoys long walks on the beach, breaking dancing, reading the sunday funnies, and eating pistachio ice cream.

Elsa "The Bus"

Elsa was named after Elsa Lanchester the star of the Bride of Frankenstein, one of the swellest movies ever that has a special place in my heart. She was used for many years to transport snotty faced children before finally being freed from her life of turmoil and degradation. Now she has embraced her new life as a magical vessel in which she now carries truly precious cargo.
